About Us

With a mission to care for and improve the quality of human life, Genesis Health, Sri Lanka’s leading group of private hospitals & diagnostic facilities, offers some of the country’s most advanced clinical programs. Established in 2003, Genesis Hospital Holdings PLC is now the Genesis Health Group comprising four strategically located hospitals - Asiri Central Hospitals (Colombo) and Asiri Hospitals (Kandy), Galle and Kurunagala - in four provinces with 1200+ staff. With patient experience and positive outcomes always at the core of what we do, all our hospitals are accredited by either Joint Commission International (UK) and quality management ISO certifications.
